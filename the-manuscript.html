How to Play

Find four groups of four literary words or names hidden among sixteen tiles. Each group shares a hidden connection.

Select four tiles and press Submit. If correct, they collapse into a colored row revealing the connection.

You have four attempts. The harder groups are more likely to mislead — a word that fits one category may seem to fit another.
